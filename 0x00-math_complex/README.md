## Learning Objectives

This project is devoted to do some basic operations in the real line and the complex plane.

-   Algebra operations.
-   Absolute values.
-   Cosinus and Sinus.
-   The Cartesian plane.
-   The Complex plane.
-   Real part and Imaginary part of Complex Numbers.
-   Modulus
-   Argument
